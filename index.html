EduProLink

Bridging Academia & Career: The Future of Integrated Learning & Professional Advancement.

EduProLink is a pioneering platform merging academic resource sharing (like Studocu) with professional networking (like LinkedIn) to empower students and lecturers globally. This infographic highlights the market, opportunity, and strategy.

  • Emphasis on lifelong learning

Monetization Model 💸

A multi-tier model with a freemium core, designed for sustainability and value delivery to all user segments.

Free Tier 🆓

(Students & Lecturers)

Limited document access/uploads, basic profiles & search. Essential for user acquisition.

Student Pro 🚀

($7-12/month)

Unlimited docs, AI study tools, ad-free. Focus: Academic Excellence.

Student Career Accelerator 💼

($18-29/month)

All Pro + advanced networking, career content, job recs. Focus: Career Development.

Lecturer Pro ✍️

($15-25/month)

Unlimited uploads for sale, lower commission, advanced analytics. Focus: Enhanced Monetization.

Lecturer Brand & Influence 📢

($30-50/month)

All Pro + advanced profile, host paid webinars, priority support. Focus: Reputation & PR.

Institutional Package 🏛️

(Custom Annual)

Campus-wide premium access, LMS integration, career services tools. Focus: Broad Scale & Impact.
